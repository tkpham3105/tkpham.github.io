About Me

TK is an MPhil student at HKUST, advised by Prof. Qifeng Chen in the Visual Intelligence Lab. Previously, he obtained the Bachelor Degree of Science from HKUST with First Class Honours, with double major in Data Science and in Computer Science. Currently, his research interests involve Diffusion-based AIGC, Autonomous Driving, and Visual Perception Understanding.
